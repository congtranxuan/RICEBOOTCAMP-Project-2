Life Expectancy Analysis

Life expectancy on a global scale varies tremendously. Using data from the World Health Organization and World Bank, we have distinguished three of the most imperative factors that influence life expectancy. These are as follows: health expenditure as a percentage of GDP, access to improved drinking water sources, and access to improved sanitation facilities.
